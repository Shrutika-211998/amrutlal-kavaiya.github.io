Mental Health multilingual Chatbot

Develop a Mental Health Chatbot designed to provide empathetic support in multiple languages, including Hindi and English, for users seeking mental health assistance. Leveraging advanced NLP, the chatbot will handle conversations with cultural sensitivity, addressing topics like stress, anxiety, and emotional well-being. Key features include personalized greetings, mood tracking, and a secure, confidential data environment, offering users a safe and accessible space to discuss mental health concerns in their preferred language.

Functionality

  • Multilingual support (165 + languages)
  • Empathetic conversation handling
  • Personalized greetings
  • Mood tracking
  • Secure and confidential data environment
  • Addressing stress, anxiety, and emotional well-being
