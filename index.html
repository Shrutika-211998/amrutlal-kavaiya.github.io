Projects

MediRAG - Intelligent Healthcare Solutions

MediRAG is an intelligent healthcare chatbot utilizing Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) to provide accurate and contextually relevant answers to medical queries. The solution is designed to enhance accessibility to healthcare information, providing quick responses to patient questions about medical conditions, treatment options, and healthcare services.

Legal Assist Chatbot

The Legal Assist Chatbot is a multilingual conversational AI tool designed to provide legal information and summarize court documents in English and various scheduled Indian languages. It leverages a multi-stage Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) architecture to deliver precise legal information, using semantic search, vector databases, and large language models (LLMs) for accurate and context-aware responses. Medical Office Receptionist Chatbot

This chatbot serves as a receptionist-like virtual assistant for medical offices. It schedules, modifies, and cancels appointments, sends SMS notifications, and integrates with Google Calendar to ensure real-time availability. Built on Google Cloud architecture, it leverages Dialogflow CX, Cloud Functions, and Twilio for SMS integration. Live POC

BankBuddy - Your AI-Powered Banking Assistant

BankBuddy is an AI-powered chatbot ecosystem for banking, providing comprehensive services such as fraud detection, personalized financial advice, and handling customer queries. The solution integrates Google Cloud technologies, including Dialogflow CX, Vertex AI, and BigQuery, to deliver personalized banking experiences. It offers features such as account management, loan eligibility assessment, and personalized financial planning. The system is built to provide financial inclusion and enhanced customer experience.

IT Support Contact Center Solution

The IT Support Contact Center Solution utilizes Google Cloud technologies to create a scalable and efficient IT support chatbot. It integrates Dialogflow CX for user inquiries, Vertex AI for intent classification, and Cloud Run for backend logic. This solution provides 24/7 support, automates routine IT tasks, and routes complex inquiries to the appropriate human agents, enhancing both customer and agent experiences. Live Demo
